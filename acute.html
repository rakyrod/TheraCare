ACUTE BACK PAIN PROGRAMME

The Acute Back Pain Programme is a specialized rehabilitation plan designed to help individuals manage and recover from sudden or severe back pain.
This program focuses on reducing pain, improving mobility, and preventing future injuries through a combination of targeted therapies, exercises, and expert guidance.

  • Pain Management: Techniques to alleviate discomfort and inflammation.

  • Personalized Exercises: Stretching and strengthening routines to enhance spinal health.

  • Posture Correction: Education on proper posture to prevent strain and re-injury.

  • Manual Therapy: Hands-on treatment to improve mobility and relieve muscle tension.

  • Expert Supervision: Guidance from trained physical therapists for safe and effective recovery.
